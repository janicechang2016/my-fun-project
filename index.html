Alpacas

Why are alpacas so great?

  1. Alpacas were domesticated by the Incas more than 6,000 years ago and raised for their exquisite fleece.
  2. Their fiber is much like sheep's wool, but warmer and not itchy; and it is hypoallergenic!
  3. Their fiber comes in 22 colors and hundreds of shades, from white to light rose gray to dark fawn, so there's no need for pollution-intensive dyeing.
